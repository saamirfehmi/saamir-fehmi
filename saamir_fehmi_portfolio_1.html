Who I Am

Building things that move.

I'm a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Ottawa (BASc, Co-op) with a focus on automotive systems, precision fabrication, and emerging technology. I'm driven by the question of how complex mechanical systems come together — and how to make them better.

On the uOttawa Baja SAE team, I work hands-on with suspension design and fabrication — machining components on a lathe, welding structural assemblies, and contributing to a vehicle that competed at Baja SAE New York 2026. That experience of building a real machine from raw material to competition is what engineering means to me.

Outside of engineering, I hold a Transport Canada Advanced Drone Pilot Licence, have applied it professionally in commercial roof inspection, and am working toward my PPL. I'm actively seeking engineering co-op placements starting May 2027.
